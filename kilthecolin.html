Welcome to Kill the Colin!

A fun, short, and joke of a platformer game made with the sole intent of making my friends laugh.

Meet Colin!

You play as Colin; a jean hating and rock loving nerd who you need to help reach the end of the game without killing (despite the ironic name of the game!)

info

*instructions on how to play the game are located on the title screen as a blue ? book.
*right click inside of the swf (the Flash game) and there will be an option to "enter fullscreen". Click this button if you want to play fullscreen.
*email tomkelpfish@gmail.com with any questions or suggestions for the game.
